# YVR Capacity Intelligence

An independent analytical deep-dive into capacity pressure 
at Vancouver International Airport (CYVR).

## What this is

Three data-driven analyses built on public YVR Annual Reports, 
scheduled flight data and declared infrastructure specifications:

- **Passenger growth vs design capacity** — YVR crossed its 
  25M design ceiling in 2018 and is on track to hit the 30M 
  expansion limit by 2027
- **Runway utilisation** — peak-hour ATM load mapped against 
  YVR's declared IFR capacity of 70 movements per hour
- **Terminal pressure by segment** — domestic, international 
  and South Terminal arrival distribution versus design share

## Data sources

YVR Annual Reports (2015–2024), AviationStack scheduled 
arrivals API, Vancouver Airport Authority public disclosures.
